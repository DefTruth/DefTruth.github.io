About

Hi! I'm DefTruth, an AI Infra engineer and open-source developer focused on making large-scale AI models run faster and cheaper.

My recent work centres on Diffusion Transformer inference acceleration — I built Cache-DiT, a PyTorch-native inference engine with hybrid caching strategies (DBCache, DBPrune, SCM) and distributed attention (UAA).

I'm also deeply invested in CUDA kernel engineering: writing HGEMM, FlashAttention, and 200+ kernels from scratch using Tensor Cores, MMA, and CuTe, documented in LeetCUDA.

I also built lite.ai.toolkit - a production C++ inference toolkit for 100+ vision models with ONNXRuntime, MNN, and TRT, ffpa-attn, torchlm, and core contributed to PaddlePaddle's FastDeploy v1.0 covering 160+ text and vision models. I am also a contributor to SGLang, vLLM, and Diffusers, focused on DiT Caching, Parallelism, Quantization and CUDA kernels.

I curate high-quality reading lists: Awesome-LLM-Inference, Awesome-DiT-Inference and lihang-notes.

I maintain the xlite-dev organisation, write on 知乎, and am ranked among top open-source developers on Trendshift.
