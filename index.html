About Me

Hi! I'm DefTruth, an AI inference engineer and open-source enthusiast passionate about deploying deep-learning models at scale.

My main focus is on high-performance C++ inference toolkits for computer vision — covering tasks like object detection, face reconstruction, image matting, super-resolution, and more.

I enjoy diving deep into hardware-level optimisations, from CUDA PTX assembly to ARM SVE/SVE2 SIMD intrinsics, making models run faster on every platform.

Beyond inference engineering, I write technical articles on 知乎 about CUDA, ONNX, and computer-vision algorithms — sharing what I learn with the Chinese ML community.

I maintain the xlite-dev organisation on GitHub, home to lightweight and production-ready AI inference libraries.

Ranked among top open-source developers on Trendshift.
